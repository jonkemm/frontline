Straight From The Frontline

Straight from the Frontline’ is a campaign to fund and support Ukrainian and international freelance journalists reporting from Ukraine

They are risking their lives to tell the truth of this war. Many are being targeted by Russian death squads.

Funds raised will provide journalists with:

  • Financial grants
  • Critical equipment – cameras, computers, body armour, combat first aid and satellite communications.
  • A 24/7 newsroom providing logistical support and coordinating international content distribution.
